## Summary
- Claude Fable is a "lobotomized" version of the Mythos 5 model—same underlying weights but with classifier models that block queries touching cybersecurity, biology, chemistry, or model distillation.
- Available on paid Claude plans until June 22nd 2026, after which it reverts to per-token billing at $50/M output tokens (2x Opus price).
- Early reviews from developers are highly positive; Fable demonstrated impressive SVG generation and Tinder-style animations.

## Key Points
- **Fable = Mythos with a muzzle**: Classifier models watch every query. Dangerous topics → query routed to Claude Opus 4.8 instead. Chinese models cannot distill a Fable-class model from this.
- **Pricing**: $50/M output tokens (vs $25/M for Opus 4.8). Free trial on paid Claude plan until June 22nd 2026.
- **Underlying model**: Literally the same weights as Mythos 5; the only difference is the safety layer.
- **Marketing genius**: Temporary free trial on paid plans → FOMO subscriptions → revenue spike before Anthropic IPO.
- **Context**: SpaceX IPO also happening this week. Anthropic filing for trillion-dollar IPO.
- **Naming observation**: "Mythos" (myth) and "Fable" (fictional story)—Anthropic's naming implies these models might be aspirational, not delivered.
- **The Google 2003 analogy**: Anthropic vs OpenAI is like Google vs Yahoo—Anthropic is the dominant force now.
- **Coding performance**: Embarrasses GPT 5.5 on benchmarks.
- **SVG generation**: Fable generated an SVG horse that actually looks like a horse + Tinder-style swipe animations.
- **The demand-to-pay popup**: After 20 minutes of work, Fable asked for more money to continue—user described it as "being scammed by a developer on Elance."
