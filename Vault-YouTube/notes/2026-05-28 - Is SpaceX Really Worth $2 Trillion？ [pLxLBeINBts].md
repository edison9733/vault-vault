## Summary
- SpaceX is assessed as a phenomenal business with a wide economic moat across four divisions: Starlink, Launch Services, Starshield, and Starship.
- 2025 revenue: $15–16B; EBITDA: $8B (EBITDA positive but not GAAP profitable).
- Projected 2030: $50B revenue, $25B EBITDA (25.6% CAGR).

## Key Points
- **Starlink** (60% of revenue): Global high-speed satellite internet with 9M+ subscribers; high-margin recurring revenue.
- **Launch Services** (dominant): Controls **80% of all commercial mass to orbit**—a current monopoly.
- **Starshield**: Secure satellite network for defense and national security; military-grade capabilities.
- **Starship**: Fully reusable large-capacity rocket; potential for deep space missions and space-based data centers.
- **Economic moat**: Wide competitive advantage far ahead of rivals.
- **GAAP vs EBITDA**: Not GAAP profitable, but EBITDA positive at $8B.
- **5-year projection**: 25.6% CAGR in profits to reach $25B EBITDA by 2030.
