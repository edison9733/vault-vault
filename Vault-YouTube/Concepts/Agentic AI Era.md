# Agentic AI Era

**What it is** - Google's term for the 2026 paradigm shift in which AI models act as autonomous agents — taking multi-step actions across apps and services — rather than as passive assistants that answer single questions. Agents are embedded in every product surface: Search, Gmail, Android, IDE tools, and glasses. Declared at Google I/O 2026 by Sundar Pichai and Demis Hassabis.

**Why it matters** - The shift from "AI that answers" to "AI that acts" changes infrastructure requirements drastically. AI agents interact with external systems (including code repositories, CI/CD pipelines, APIs) at machine speed and volume — far exceeding the traffic patterns that existing platforms like GitHub were designed for.

**Example** - GitHub's CTO admitted in April 2026 that "agentic development workflows have accelerated sharply since 2025," identifying AI agent traffic as the primary cause of GitHub's infrastructure failures. The same capability enabling productivity also enables unprecedented platform load.
