# Escrow Program

**What it is** - A [[Smart Contract]] that holds assets (tokens, NFTs, or other on-chain value) in temporary custody until a defined condition is met, then automatically releases them to the correct party. It is the on-chain equivalent of a neutral third party holding money in trust.

**Why it matters** - Escrow is one of the most foundational patterns in [[DeFi]] and Web3 development. It enables trustless transactions between strangers: neither party needs to trust the other because the blockchain contract enforces the rules and executes the transfer automatically when conditions are satisfied.

**Example** - In a [[DEX]] token swap, an escrow contract holds Token A from the seller until the buyer deposits Token B. When both sides fulfill their obligations, the contract releases each token to the appropriate party simultaneously — no bank or intermediary required.
