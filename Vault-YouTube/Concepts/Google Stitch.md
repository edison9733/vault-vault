# Google Stitch

**What it is** — Google's AI-powered UI design tool, released in early 2026. Generates static UI screens and layouts from text prompts or uploaded design files. Faster generation speed than [[Claude Design]] but produces less interactive output.

**Why it matters** — Direct competitor to [[Claude Design]]; the comparison reveals the trade-off between speed (Stitch wins) and interactivity (Claude Design wins). Covered in its own Fireship video a few weeks before the Claude Design release.

**Example** — Google Stitch generates a multi-screen app layout from a prompt in seconds; Claude Design generates the same but with fully interactive, animated transitions and shader effects — at the cost of 5–10 minutes per generation pass.
