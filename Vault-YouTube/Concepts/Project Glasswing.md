# Project Glasswing

**What it is** - Anthropic's initiative to give selective access to [[Claude Mythos]] — their withheld flagship model — to a coalition of large enterprise customers (described as "trillion-dollar companies and a bank"). The stated goal is to use Mythos's vulnerability-finding power to patch the world's critical software before adversaries build a comparably capable model.

**Why it matters** - It reframes a safety decision (withholding a dangerous model) as a business partnership: the companies gaining access are existing Anthropic customers. Critics note this creates a two-tier system where Mythos's offensive capabilities are available to large corporations but not to independent researchers or defenders.

**Example** - Announced April 2026 alongside the public disclosure of Mythos; access is limited to enterprise partners, with no independent audit or verification mechanism.
