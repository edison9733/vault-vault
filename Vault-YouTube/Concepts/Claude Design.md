# Claude Design

**What it is** — An AI-powered UI/UX design platform from [[Anthropic]], released April 2026 and built on [[Opus 4.7]]. Takes text prompts, Figma files, or uploaded design systems and generates fully interactive prototypes, pitch decks, and production-ready UIs without requiring a separate design tool.

**Why it matters** — Competes directly with [[Google Stitch]] and collapses the design-to-prototype workflow from days to minutes. Differentiator: Claude Design generates fully interactive outputs (working animations, shader effects, video animations over a minute long) where competitors produce static layouts. Caused Figma stock to drop 7% on announcement.

**Example** — Upload a 2-screen Figma design system, prompt Claude Design to build an iOS onboarding flow → receive 5 fully interactive screens with animations in ~5–10 minutes; draw annotations on the canvas to trigger targeted regeneration passes.
