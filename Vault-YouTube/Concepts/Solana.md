# Solana

**What it is** - A public blockchain network designed for very high speed and low transaction costs. Unlike older blockchains, Solana can process tens of thousands of transactions per second by using a mechanism called Proof of History to order events without slowing down the network.

**Why it matters** - Solana has become a major platform for decentralized finance ([[DeFi]]) and trading. Its speed makes it attractive to professional trading firms that need near-instant settlement, something older blockchains cannot provide.

**Example** - Solana processed over $1 trillion in transaction volume in 2025. Co-founder Anatoly Yakovenko's stated vision is that Solana becomes the open infrastructure of global finance — "like Linux, but for money."
