# Gemma 4

**What it is** - Google's open-source large language model family released in April 2026 under the [[Apache 2.0 License]]. The flagship 31B parameter version runs on a single consumer GPU (RTX 4090, 20 GB download) while scoring at or near frontier-class benchmarks. Smaller "E" variants (E2B, E4B) use per-layer embeddings for even greater efficiency.

**Why it matters** - Gemma 4 is the first model from a major US tech company to combine genuine Apache 2.0 openness, frontier-competitive intelligence, and consumer-hardware accessibility in a single package — challenging the assumption that powerful open models must be enormous.

**Example** - The 31B Gemma 4 scores in the same benchmark ballpark as Kimmi K 2.5 thinking (which requires 600+ GB download and multiple H100s), running at ~10 tokens/sec on a single RTX 4090 via Ollama.
