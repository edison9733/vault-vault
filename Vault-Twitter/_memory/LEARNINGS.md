- [2026-06-12] Giveaway, airdrop, and "drop your wallet" tweets always score 0-2.
- [2026-06-12] A thread's first tweet is scored by the substance of the whole thread, not its hook line.
- [2026-06-12] When multiple accounts RT the same announcement (e.g., Frontier Traders, $SPCX), score the most-informative version 7+ and the bare repost 6; duplicate signal should not inflate the digest.
- [2026-06-12] @solana RTs of live-stream broadcast links with no surrounding context always score ≤3 (no news content, just a tap-to-watch link).
- [2026-06-12] Teaser tweets with no informational content ("Something is cooking", "Wow @X") always score ≤2, regardless of account reliability — wait for the announcement tweet.
- [2026-06-12] @federalreserve: named statistical releases (G.19 consumer credit, Z.1 flow of funds) score 7-8; routine weekly link tweets (H.8, H.4.1) with no headline numbers score 4-5; governor speech announcements score 6-7; advance notice of a vice chair testimony (day before) scores 6 and live testimony scores 7 — include both in the digest but flag the advance notice as context only (they are one event, not two); educational FAQs, calendar links, and trivia RTs score ≤4.
- [2026-06-12] First-milestone tweets for a DeFi primitive (first major loan, first leveraged product live) score 7-8 even when the protocol is young; the milestone is the news, not the protocol's maturity.
